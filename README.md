# Football U22 Players Analysis

## Overview

This repository hosts an in-depth analysis of Under-22 (U22) football players, focusing on metrics such as appearances, average appearances per season, goal contributions, and market value. The dataset used in this analysis is sourced from Kaggle, specifically the [Player Scores Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/davidcariboo/player-scores/data).

## Objectives

1. **Identify Emerging Talents:** Scrutinize the performance metrics of U22 players to identify emerging talents with noteworthy contributions.

2. **Long-term Investments:** Uncover players with relatively low market values but high goal contributions and substantial appearances, suggesting their potential as long-term investments.

3. **Comparison with Superstars:** Analyze how emerging talents compare with established superstars in terms of market value, appearances, and goal contributions.

4. **Market Value Changes:** Examine the historical market value changes of U22 players, identifying trends and potential factors influencing their value evolution.

5. **Player Recommendations:** Provide insights and recommendations on promising U22 players who offer a favorable balance of performance metrics and market value.

## Key Findings

### 1. Emerging Talents
   - Promising players valued under 20 million EUR, such as Kevin Kelsy and Sergey Volkov, showcase potential with over 30 appearances per season.

### 2. Cost-Effective Contributions
   - U22 players like Brian Brobbey, Konstantin Tyukavin, and Elye Wahi deliver significant goal contributions at a more affordable cost compared to established names.

### 3. Superstar Trends
   - Established superstars like Jude Bellingham and Jamal Musiala exhibit continuous growth in market value, while anomalies like Ansu Fati's decline prompt further exploration.

### 4. Rapid Value Emergence
   - Players like Warren Zaire-Emery and Lamine Yamal demonstrate rapid value growth, contrasting with others like Evan Ferguson, who exhibit steady growth over time.
